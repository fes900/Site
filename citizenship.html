Some Components of Citizenship!

Citizenship means that you must be respectful of everyone and everything. Also, you must be responsible. Honesty: Honesty means that you must tell the truth and not lie. Also, you must take responsibility in your actions.

Compassion: Compassion means that what ever you want to achieve, you must work hard and thoughtfully.

Respect: Respect means that you have to show that you care about other people. You must treat them nicely.

Responsibility: Responsibility means that if you did something wrong, then you have to be honest about your actions.

Courage: Courage means that you have to sometimes have to step out of your comfort zone to be able to achieve something.

Trustworthy: The ability of being honest or truthful about something.
